Level Calculation — Quick Formula
Signal Price = Close of trigger candle
Price 24h ago = Signal ÷ (1 + gain% ÷ 100)
Entry = Signal × 0.96 (or 0.94 if gain 60–100% · 0.92 if 100%+)
Stop = Signal × 1.07 (7% above signal · ~11.5% from entry)
Target = Entry − (Entry − P24ago) × 0.60
Example
Signal = $1.00 · Gain = 40% · P24ago = $0.714
Entry = $0.960 · Stop = $1.070 · Target = $0.789
